## 💚 About Ecostars 

Ecostars is a sustainability platform for hotels and hotel groups. We help hospitality companies measure, improve, certify and communicate their sustainability performance through technology, data and practical guidance.

We are a lean, fast-moving startup building products that solve real sustainability challenges for the hospitality sector. As we continue to expand our platform and capabilities, we are looking for a Sustainability Lead to become our internal ESG expert and help shape the future of our product and market positioning.

## 🫵 About you - Role overview

You will be the sustainability reference within the company.

Your mission is to ensure that Ecostars stays ahead of relevant sustainability regulations, market trends and best practices, while helping translate complex ESG requirements into practical solutions for hotels and hotel groups.

This is not a Product Manager role. Instead, you will work closely with Product, Engineering, Customer Success and Leadership teams, providing domain expertise and strategic guidance.

## Responsibilities

### Product & Regulatory Advisory

* Support Product and Leadership teams with sustainability expertise and regulatory interpretation.
* Help identify, prioritize and anticipate upcoming ESG requirements that may impact customers.
* Translate regulations, standards and frameworks into product opportunities and requirements.
* Monitor developments in sustainability regulations and reporting frameworks across different industries and geographies.
* Provide domain expertise during product discovery, roadmap discussions and feature definition.

### Internal Enablement

* Deliver sustainability training sessions for internal teams.
* Build internal knowledge and awareness around ESG topics.
* Act as the go-to person for sustainability-related questions across the company.

### Content & Thought Leadership

* Create sustainability content for customers, prospects and partners.
* Develop practical guidance explaining ESG concepts and how Ecostars addresses them.
* Support the creation of reports, whitepapers, articles and educational materials.
* Ensure consistency and accuracy across sustainability communications.

### External Representation

* Represent Ecostars in webinars, conferences, industry events and customer meetings.
* Support commercial and customer-facing teams with sustainability expertise.
* Participate in industry discussions and help position Ecostars as a trusted sustainability partner for hospitality organizations.
